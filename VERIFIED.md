## Verification Criteria

A verified place includes:

1. **Historical Context** — When built, significant events, key people
2. **Haunting Origins** — What tragedy sparked the haunting, who died, how
3. **Named Ghosts** — Specific spirits with names and backstories
4. **Paranormal Activity** — Detailed phenomena, locations within the site
5. **Witness Accounts** — Specific experiences with dates when available
6. **Investigation Findings** — TAPS, Ghost Adventures, or local group results
7. **5+ Independent Sources** — Cross-referenced and verified
